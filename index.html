Top Tips for Winning

  • Master Map Awareness: Always keep an eye on the minimap to anticipate enemy movements.
  • Farm Efficiently: Prioritize farming to gain gold and experience quickly.
  • Team Communication: Use pings and chat to coordinate with your team.
  • Hero Positioning: Know where your hero should be during team fights.
  • Item Builds: Adapt your item builds to counter the enemy team's strategy.
